The Creators

Richard Hess

I am currently a chemistry major studying in the University of Wyoming. Before transferring to UWyo I spent two years getting my associates degree in Central Wyoming College. During that time I discovered a love for chemistry. This year I am studying inorganic chemistry. While I am currently working my way towards my bachelors in chemistry I also intend to earn one in chemical engineering.

I am still not entirely sure what I want to do with myself when I graduate. I have participated in several research projects in the past and I enjoyed them so that is a possibility. This past year I have tutored other students and found that I liked it. I may get a degree in education so that I can become a teacher. Regardless of what I decide to do, in the end it will in some way involve chemistry.

Christopher Larsen

I have been an undergrad in computer science for a little over two years now. Throughout my life I have tutored kids and been programming captain for many clubs. Through the work I have done with tutoring kids I eventually became a software coach for a high school robotics club and help the Wyoming Board of Education with implementing computer science from kindergarten to senior year of high school.

After my years of experience I found that many kids struggle finding resources to understand the programming language they are using. I hope to releive this issue by hosting a website that can take kids to other creditable sites.
