I have been an undergrad in computer science for a little over two years now. Throughout my life I have tutored kids and been programming captain for many clubs. Through the work I have done with tutoring kids I eventually became a software coach for a high school robotics club and help the Wyoming Board of Education with implementing computer science from kindergarten to senior year of high school.

After my years of experience I found that many kids struggle finding resources to understand the programming language they are using. I hope to releive this issue by hosting a website that can take kids to other creditable sites.
