Who We Are

In our experience it can be difficult to find useful resources for chemistry and computer science. It may take hours for us to find a site that further explores our areas of expertise with reliable and credible information. Google is an incredible tool that will greatly expedite the search for information, but it is only useful if you know what you are searching for. Our website is designed so that a variety of useful resources, some you may not have even known you needed, can all be found in one spot. This will allow others to avoid the problems that we encountered. Every resource found on our website is one that we have used in the past and would recommend to anyone else in our field.
